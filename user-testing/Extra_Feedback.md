## OPTIONAL: Do you have any feedback for this app?

UX - needs clearer separation between resident and staff log in routing

**Staff View dashboard**

**1) Summary Tiles (Waiting in Reception, Stepped Out, etc.)**
x4 tiles at top of screen (waiting in reception, stepped out etc) imply they are clickable to provide filtered views, but aren't. Would be intuitive to act as filters to allow fast navigation to the category/info they represent.

**2) Manage Tickets**
Needs to include date/time 'ticket' was created and method to sort into (at least) date asc/desc order. Would benefit from displaying more Info in each 'ticket' tile. Expected tiles to be clickable to display detail of individual request. Using View/Edit Notes - added dummy test and saved. This is not visible either on the ticket as displayed here nor (that I can see) in the ticket detail when viewed under Case Management. What is it's purpose?

**3) Case Management**
Whilst this functions as I'd expect Manage Tickets would, addition of sortation by date/time would be a useful addition. Addition of detail (without clicking) even headline, like resident name, would greatly improve usability when a staff member is trying to locate a particular request. When an individual ticket is selected and status changed (open/in progress/resolved/closed) it would be good to capture date/time/who made this change (audit trail)
